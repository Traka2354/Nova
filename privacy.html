Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Sonic AI CopyX ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you visit our website at sonicaicopyx.com or interact with our services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

By using this website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of this website.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide

We do not operate a user account system on this website. However, if you contact us directly, we may collect:

  • Your name and email address (when you contact us by email)
  • Any information you voluntarily share in communications

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain technical data:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location (country/city level)
  • Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on page, and navigation paths
  • Referring URL (how you arrived at our site)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

2.3 Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and browser local storage for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the site to function (e.g., storing your cookie consent preference)
  • Analytics cookies: Used by Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (only set if you accept cookies)
  • Preference cookies: Storing your language preference (HR/EN)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate and improve this website and its content
  • To understand how visitors interact with our site (analytics)
  • To respond to your enquiries and communications
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. A cookie is a small text file stored on your device. You can control cookies through your browser settings or through our cookie consent banner.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • cookieConsent — Stores your cookie preference (accepted/declined). Session-persistent.
  • siteLang — Stores your language preference (en/hr). Session-persistent.
  • Google Analytics (_ga, _gid, _gat) — Used to measure site traffic and usage. Only set if you accept cookies.

If you decline cookies, we will not set analytics cookies. Essential cookies (consent and language preference) are stored in localStorage and are necessary for the site to function correctly.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right of access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): You can request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to restriction of processing: You can request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to object: You can object to the processing of your data for certain purposes.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at ktrakostanec@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
